This ROM displays a single color at a time for diagnostic purposes.

Should work on both NTSC or PAL.


Controls:

Left/Right - Select low bits of color (chroma)
Up/Down    - Select high bits of color (luma)
B+Left     - toggle blue emphasis
B+Up       - toggle green emphasis (red on PAL)
B+Right    - toggle red emphasis (green on PAL)
B+Down     - turn off all emphasis
A          - toggle greyscale
Select     - toggle display
